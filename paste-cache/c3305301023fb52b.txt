## Practical takeaway
If your screener use stays within free-plan limits, a paid account isn’t required; if you need higher request limits, more history, or premium datasets, then upgrading is necessary. [site.financialmodelingprep](https://site.financialmodelingprep.com/developer/docs/pricing)
