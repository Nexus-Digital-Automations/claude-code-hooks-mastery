## Metric Definitions

| Metric | Definition | Good Value |
|--------|------------|------------|
| **Sharpe Ratio** | Risk-adjusted return (excess return / volatility) | > 1.5 excellent |
| **Sortino Ratio** | Downside risk-adjusted return (only penalizes losses) | > 2.0 excellent |
| **Volatility** | Annualized standard deviation of returns | < 20% moderate |
| **Max Drawdown** | Largest peak-to-trough decline | < 20% good |
| **Beta** | Correlation/sensitivity to SPY | < 0.5 = defensive |
| **Quality Score** | Profitability, stability, financial health (0-100) | > 70 = high quality |
| **Momentum Score** | Price trend strength (0-100) | > 70 = strong |
